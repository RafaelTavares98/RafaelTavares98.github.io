Rafael Tavares

Data Analyst

I am a Data Analyst who combines a practical background in business operations with strong technical skills in Excel, SQL, Power BI, and Python. I focus on transforming raw datasets into clear metrics, automating redundant workflows, and building analytical models to solve operational bottlenecks.

During my time at Coty, I collaborated directly with the North Carolina operations team. To address their manual reporting bottlenecks, I conducted operational benchmarks and built automated data solutions using Python, Excel and VBA. This allowed the regional team to bypass manual data entry, evaluate performance metrics faster, and directly improve their daily workflow efficiency.

To expand my technical capabilities into machine learning and system deployment, I actively participated in an MLOps study group at the Federal University of Goiás (UFG), where we developed end-to-end projects and applied core model deployment principles. Additionally, as a researcher within UFG's AKCIT group, I studied Reinforcement Learning and implemented advanced algorithms—including DQN, PPO, and DIDEC—to solve complex optimization environments.

I am now applying this mix of practical business analytics and advanced academic research to Data Analyst roles. My primary focus is to build efficient data architectures, automate repetitive tasks, and deliver accurate insights that directly support daily business operations.
